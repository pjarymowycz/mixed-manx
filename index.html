About This Site:

At first glance, life in NYC may seem disconnected from the natural world.
However, a closer look shows the city is full of parks, green spaces, and community gardens for all New Yorkers to enjoy!

New York City administers the vast majority of public parks and green spaces within city limits, although several state- and nationally-run parks can also be found here. Additional information about city parks can be obtained from the New York City Department of Parks & Recreation

Community gardens are also prevalent throughout the city. According to the NYC Department of Parks and Recreation, the NYC GreenThumb program is the largest community gardening program in the nation with over 500 registered community gardens.
